The goal of my portfolio is to promote my approachability. I love talking professionally with others, especially making good ties with those I am going to work with. The goal is to use this digital portfolio to apply for internships or freelance work, showing I am capable of building a web page that is coordinated, professional, and always with the client in mind. I want to showcase my past work to an extent, but put an emphasis on how I will continue to work in the future.
